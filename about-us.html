Rishik Hospitals

Rajasthan's First Dedicated Center of Bariatric, Cosmetic & Gastro Sciences

Rishik Hospitals located at Vidhyadhar Nagar, Jaipur is Rajasthan’s First Dedicated Center Of Bariatric, Cosmetic & Gastro Sciences. The hospital offers end-to-end clinical, surgical and diagnostic services. Rishik Hospitals offers all type of advanced, Bariatric Surgeries, Cosmetic Surgeries, Endoscopic procedures, Hepatobiliary and Liver Surgeries and Transplants. The hospital has round-the-clock services for Emergencies, Critical Care and Trauma.

Ideal Hospital for in-patient and out-patient care with the latest state-of-art infrastructure in Modular Operation Theatres and well-equipped wards and ICU.

One of Rajasthan’s best Center for all Bariatric Surgeries and Cosmetic Surgeries including the latest techniques of single/incision/no scar surgeries.

Our Vision and Mission

To enhance the health and well-being of our community by providing compassionate, high-quality healthcare services through dedicated professionals and advanced medical practices.

Mission

At Rishik Hospital, our mission is to provide exceptional healthcare services specializing in bariatric, cosmetic, and gastroenterology treatments. We are committed to delivering personalized, innovative, and compassionate care to improve the quality of life for every patient. Through advanced medical technology and a patient-first approach, we aim to set new benchmarks in healthcare excellence.

Vision

To become a leading center of excellence in bariatric, cosmetic, and gastroenterology healthcare, recognized globally for our clinical expertise, cutting-edge technology, and patient-centric services. We strive to inspire confidence and promote wellness by transforming lives with innovative solutions and ethical medical practices.

Values

At Rishik Hospital, our values guide everything we do. We are committed to compassionate care, delivering excellence through innovation and integrity. Our patient-centric approach ensures dignity and respect for every individual, while teamwork and sustainability drive us to create lasting health and wellness for our community.
