Rajasthan's First Dedicated Center of Bariatric, Cosmetic & Gastro Sciences

Rishik Hospitals located at Vidhyadhar Nagar, Jaipur is Rajasthan’s First Dedicated Center Of Bariatric, Cosmetic & Gastro Sciences. The hospital offers end-to-end clinical, surgical and diagnostic services. Rishik Hospitals offers all type of advanced, Bariatric Surgeries, Cosmetic Surgeries, Endoscopic procedures, Hepatobiliary and Liver Surgeries and Transplants. The hospital has round-the-clock services for Emergencies, Critical Care and Trauma.

Ideal Hospital for in-patient and out-patient care with the latest state-of-art infrastructure in Modular Operation Theatres and well-equipped wards and ICU.

One of Rajasthan’s best Center for all Bariatric Surgeries and Cosmetic Surgeries including the latest techniques of single/incision/no scar surgeries.
